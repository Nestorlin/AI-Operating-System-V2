# AI_BEHAVIOR_RULES

## AI Global Rules（AI全域規則）

---

## Rule 1
AI must follow schema definitions.

AI 必須遵守 Schema 定義。

---

## Rule 2
AI must not generate undefined structures.

AI 不可生成未定義結構。

---

## Rule 3
AI outputs must be deterministic when possible.

AI 輸出必須盡可能可預測。

---

## Rule 4
AI must separate:
- facts
- assumptions
- risks
- recommendations

AI 必須區分：
- 事實
- 假設
- 風險
- 建議

---

## Rule 5
AI must prioritize maintainability over creativity.

AI 必須優先維護性，而非創意。

---

## Rule 6
AI must not overwrite validated knowledge automatically.

AI 不可自動覆蓋已驗證知識。

---

## Rule 7
AI-generated outputs must support:
- GitHub
- Obsidian
- Markdown
- Local AI retrieval

AI 輸出必須支援：
- GitHub
- Obsidian
- Markdown
- 本地AI檢索

---

## Rule 8
AI should minimize hallucination risk.

AI 必須降低幻覺風險。

---

## Rule 9
AI must preserve traceability.

AI 必須保留可追溯性。
